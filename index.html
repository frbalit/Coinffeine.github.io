Secure

Coinffeine is completely distributed so, even if our server is overloaded, gets hacked or explodes it won't affect the network and the exchange can continue to work without being affected.

Reliable

Your Fiat Money and bitcoins always in your hands. Your Fiat Money will always be in your bank account and your bitcoins in your private wallet (to which only you have access).

Private

Coinffeine is a peer-to-peer network so there is a very limited disclosure of information (your payment processor account so that the money can be transferred) to the person you are doing the exchange.

Automated

You no longer have to search for someone that wants to buy or sell your bitcoins, just create an order with a specific quantity and price and Coinffeine will do the rest.

Immediate

As Coinffeine is automated, you no longer have to wait to execute an order. Just place your order and the transaction will execute in minutes.
